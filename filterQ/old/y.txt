
yard [jɑrd] (n.) 碼；院子
She has a beautiful garden in her yard. (她的院子裡有一個美麗的花園。)

year [jɪr] (n.) 年；年度
I was born in the year 1990. (我出生在1990年。)

yellow [ˈjɛlo] (adj./n.) 黃色的；黃色
The sunflower has yellow petals. (向日葵有黃色的花瓣。)

yes [jɛs] (adv.) 是的；是啊
'Do you want some more cake?' 'Yes, please.' ('你想要再來一塊蛋糕嗎？' '是的，謝謝'。)

yesterday [ˈjɛstɚdeɪ] (adv./n.) 昨天；過去
Yesterday's news was very interesting. (昨天的新聞很有趣。)

yet [jɛt] (adv./conj.) 還沒；但是；然而
I haven't finished my homework yet. (我還沒做完作業。)

yield [jild] (v./n.) 生產；產出；屈服；放棄
The troops finally yielded to the enemy's attack. (部隊最終屈服於敵人的攻擊。)

young [jʌŋ] (adj./n.) 年輕的；年輕人
He is a young man with a bright future. (他是一個前途光明的年輕人。)

your [jʊr] (pron.) 你的；你們的
Is this your pen? (這是你的筆嗎？。)

youth [juθ] (n.) 年輕；青春；青年
The youth of today face many challenges. (今天的年輕人面臨著許多挑戰。)
